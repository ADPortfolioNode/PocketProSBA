SBA LOAN TYPES GUIDE - GEMINI RAG

SBA 7(A) LOANS:
Description: SBA 7(a) loans for general business purposes
Maximum Amount: $5 million
Use Cases: working capital, equipment, real estate, debt refinancing
Terms: up to 25 years for real estate, 10 years for equipment/working capital

SBA 504 LOANS:
Description: SBA 504 loans for major fixed assets
Maximum Amount: $5.5 million
Use Cases: real estate, major equipment, construction
Terms: 10, 20, or 25 years

SBA MICROLOANS:
Description: SBA Microloans for small businesses
Maximum Amount: $50,000
Use Cases: working capital, inventory, supplies, equipment
Terms: up to 6 years
